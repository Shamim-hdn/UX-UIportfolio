Auto Space Case Study

Auto Space is a cutting-edge augmented reality application designed to assist in automobile repair. This project was developed as a group effort during my university studies.

Role: UX/UI Designer and researcher

Tools Used: Figma, Sketch, Miro

Challenges: The main challenge was integrating augmented reality features into a user-friendly interface. We conducted extensive user testing to ensure the app met user needs and expectations.

Outcome: The project was highly successful, demonstrating the potential of augmented reality in practical applications. It was well-received by both users and academic evaluators.

Carb Buddy Case Study

Carb Buddy is a mobile application developed to assist individuals with diabetes in managing and monitoring their blood glucose levels. This university project focused on creating a reliable and easy-to-use tool for daily health management.

Role: UX/UI Designer

Tools Used: Sketch, Miro, Adobe XD, Figma

Challenges: Designing for a diverse user base with varying levels of tech proficiency. We conducted extensive user interviews and usability tests to ensure the app was accessible and effective.

Outcome: The app received positive feedback from users, who appreciated its simplicity and helpful features. It was recognized for its potential impact on health management.

Parent Hub Case Study

Parent Hub is an accessible website and mobile application designed to help parents with disabilities connect with their community and participate in local events. This solo project underscores my commitment to inclusive design and user-centered solutions.

Role: Sole UX Designer and Developer

Tools Used: Figma, Miro, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Challenges: Ensuring the platform was fully accessible to users with various disabilities required thorough research and testing. Adhering to WCAG guidelines was a top priority.

Outcome: The project was lauded for its accessibility features and user-friendly interface. It would help many parents engage more actively in their communities.
